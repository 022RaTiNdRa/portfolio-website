# Code of Conduct

This project follows the Contributor Covenant v2.1 to provide a welcoming and harassment-free experience for everyone.

Standards

- Be respectful and considerate in language and actions.
- Be inclusive — value diverse perspectives and experiences.
- Refrain from demeaning, discriminatory, or harassing behaviour.

Unacceptable behaviour

- Insults, name-calling, or discriminatory remarks
- Threats, stalking, or sustained harassment
- Publishing others' private information

Reporting

If you are subject to or witness unacceptable behaviour, please contact the repository maintainers at: maintainer@example.com (replace with a real contact email).

Enforcement

Reports will be reviewed and investigated, and appropriate action will be taken at the maintainers' discretion. This may include removal of comments, blocking contributors, or reporting to relevant platforms.

Acknowledgements

This Code of Conduct is adapted from the Contributor Covenant. See https://www.contributor-covenant.org/ for more information.
